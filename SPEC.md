## Task 4 — Write working tax logic

In src/utils/federalTax.ts write real working logic:
- 2024 federal brackets for single and married filing jointly
- Standard deduction: $14,600 single, $29,200 MFJ
- Traditional 401k reduces taxable income
- Roth 401k does not reduce taxable income
- Export: calcFederalTax(taxableIncome, filingStatus)

In src/utils/stateTax.ts:
- All 50 states + DC
- 9 states with no income tax: TX, FL, WA, NV, WY, SD, AK, TN, NH
- Export: calcStateTax(taxableIncome, state)

In src/utils/taxEngine.ts:
- Orchestrate: 401k deduction → federal → state → SS → Medicare → SDI
- Social Security: 6.2% capped at $168,600
- Medicare: 1.45% flat
- CA SDI: 1.1% (California only for now)
- Export: calcFullBreakdown(inputs) returning full breakdown object
